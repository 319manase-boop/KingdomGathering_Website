Apostle Nonofo Wilfred Mataboge

A visionary leader with over two decades of ministry experience, dedicated to building strong communities and expanding God's Kingdom.

Education

Founded
Kingdom Gathering Church 2018
Ministry Journey

Nonofo Wilfred Mataboge received the life of Christ in September 1996. In January 2000, he underwent Diploma in Biblical Theology and Ministry training at the Assembly Bible College. Upon completion he served as an associate pastor in Mahalapye. In 2005 he established another Assemblies of God Church branch in Mahalapye which he pastored until November 1st 2007. He was transferred to Maun on 24th November 2007 and led the congregation until 2017. In 2018, he founded Kingdom Gathering Church, focusing on discipleship and community outreach.

"Apostle Mataboge is committed to the core values of discipleship, love, generosity, prayer, excellence, and honor. He continues to lead the church with vision and dedication."

Our Leadership Values

The principles that guide our ministry and shape our community

Discipleship

Growing in faith and knowledge of God's Word through dedicated teaching and mentorship.

Love

Extending God's love to all people through compassion, service, and genuine care.

Generosity

Giving freely of time, resources, and talents to bless others and advance God's Kingdom.

Prayer

Seeking God's guidance and wisdom through fervent prayer and spiritual discipline.

Excellence

Striving for the highest standards in all aspects of ministry and service.

Honor

Respecting God, leadership, and one another with integrity and humility.

Growth

Continuously developing spiritually, personally, and as a community.

Service

Serving God and community with dedication and selflessness.
