Children's Ministry

Nurturing faith and biblical knowledge in our young members.

Click to learn more
About Children's Ministry

Our Children's Ministry is dedicated to providing a safe, fun, and spiritually enriching environment for kids aged 3-12. Through age-appropriate Bible stories, songs, crafts, and games, we help children develop a strong foundation in faith.

Activities Include:
  • Sunday School classes
  • Vacation Bible School
  • Children's Choir
  • Youth Camps

Contact: children's@kingdomgathering.org

About Women's Ministry

The Women's Ministry focuses on spiritual growth, fellowship, and service. We provide opportunities for women to connect, learn, and serve together in various community outreach programs.

Activities Include:
  • Bible study groups
  • Prayer meetings
  • Community service projects
  • Women's conferences

Contact: women@kingdomgathering.org

Men's Ministry

About Men's Ministry

Our Men's Ministry equips men to be godly leaders in their homes, workplaces, and communities. Through fellowship, discipleship, and service, we foster brotherhood and spiritual growth.

Activities Include:
  • Men's breakfast meetings
  • Discipleship groups
  • Sports and outdoor activities
  • Men's retreats

Clinic Outreach

Serving the community through health and wellness initiatives.

Click to learn more
About Clinic Outreach

The Clinic Outreach Ministry provides healthcare services and wellness education to underserved communities. We partner with local clinics and medical professionals to offer free health screenings, vaccinations, and health education.

Services Include:
  • Free health screenings
  • Vaccination drives
  • Health education workshops
  • Medical mission trips

Contact: clinic@kingdomgathering.org

Cell Groups

Small group gatherings for fellowship and spiritual growth.

Click to learn more
About Cell Groups

Cell Groups are small, intimate gatherings where members can build deep relationships, study the Bible, pray together, and support one another in their faith journey. These groups meet regularly in homes throughout the community.

What to Expect:
  • Bible study and discussion
  • Prayer time
  • Fellowship and meals
  • Accountability and support

Contact: cells@kingdomgathering.org

About Campus Outreach

Our Campus Outreach Ministry focuses on connecting with university and college students. We organize events, Bible studies, and fellowship opportunities to share the love of Christ with young adults in academic settings.

Activities Include:
  • Campus Bible studies
  • Student fellowship events
  • Career and life guidance
  • Partnerships with local universities
