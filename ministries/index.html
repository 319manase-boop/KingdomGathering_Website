Children's Ministry

Nurturing faith and biblical knowledge in our young members.

Click to learn more
About Children's Ministry
Vision

To raise a generation of children who are rooted in grace, truth, and a love for Jesus.

Mission

To nurture young hearts with biblical teaching, joyful worship, and safe mentorship so they can grow into lifelong followers of Christ.

Philosophy

We believe early spiritual formation is vital, and that children learn best through creative, age-appropriate Bible teaching, play, and caring relationships.

Core Values
  • Love: Providing a warm and welcoming environment for every child.
  • Discipleship: Teaching children to follow Jesus in simple, practical ways.
  • Faith: Helping young hearts trust God through scripture and prayer.
  • Prayer: Encouraging children to pray and depend on God.
  • Excellence: Delivering safe, joyful, and quality programs.
  • Joy: Celebrating faith with fun, creativity, and laughter.
Implementation Strategies
  • Sunday School classes with interactive Bible lessons.
  • Vacation Bible School and holiday Bible camps.
  • Children's choir and worship experiences.
  • Family-focused events and parent-child discipleship days.
Our Commitment

We are committed to providing a loving, spiritually rich environment where every child can discover Jesus and begin a lifelong faith journey.

To cultivate a generation of teenagers and youth who are rooted in grace and truth, reflecting the heart of Christ in every sphere of life.

Kingdom Arts

Expressing worship and creativity through various artistic mediums.

Click to learn more
About Kingdom Arts
Vision

To be a citadel of grace and truth where the arts serve as a divine channel for communicating God’s redemptive message, inspiring steadfast faith, and expanding the Kingdom of God.

Mission

To make disciples of Jesus Christ and develop leaders by utilizing every artistic talent to represent Christ, reconcile people to God, and embody our Kingdom values throughout the world.

Philosophy

At Kingdom Gathering Church, we believe the arts—writing, public speaking, painting, poetry, singing, dancing, architecture, drawing, sculpting, filming, photography, theatre, and music—are God’s gifts to humanity. We are committed to stewarding these gifts as strategic tools for outreach, discipleship, and spiritual formation, engaging every generation and gender.

Core Values
  • Love: Communicating Christ’s love through creative expression.
  • Discipleship: Mentoring and growing artists as followers of Christ.
  • Faith: Using the arts to inspire and strengthen faith in God.
  • Prayer: Seeking God’s guidance and anointing in every creative endeavor.
  • Generosity: Sharing artistic gifts selflessly for the benefit of others.
  • Honour: Valuing every talent and respecting the dignity of all people.
  • Excellence: Pursuing the highest standards in every artistic pursuit.
Ministry Description

Kingdom Arts is an intergenerational, inclusive ministry that harnesses creative talents to proclaim the gospel to a wide and diverse audience, encourage and exhort believers in their faith journey, represent Christ in the public square and cultural spaces, develop artists as spiritual leaders and culture-shapers, and create opportunities for collaboration, innovation, and community impact.

Implementation Strategies
  • Organizing art exhibitions, performances, and showcases.
  • Facilitating workshops and training in various art forms.
  • Integrating the arts into worship, outreach, and discipleship programs.
  • Supporting, mentoring, and discipling artists of all ages.
  • Using the arts for evangelism, apologetics, and community development.
Target Audience

Children, teenagers, youth, and adults of all genders—anyone with a creative gift or passion to serve the Kingdom of God through the arts.

Our Commitment

We are dedicated to raising and releasing Kingdom artists who embody grace and truth, boldly communicate the gospel, and advance the Kingdom of God through every creative platform.

Mamlakah Training Centre

Equipping believers for impactful Kingdom ministry and leadership.

Click to learn more
About Mamlakah Training Centre
Vision

To serve as a citadel of grace and truth, equipping believers for impactful ministry and leadership in the Kingdom of God.

Mission

To make disciples of Jesus Christ and develop leaders through comprehensive vocational and Christian education, empowering them to expand God's Kingdom in every sphere of influence.

Philosophy

Rooted in the belief that Christian education is essential to the growth and expansion of God's Kingdom, Mamlakah Training Centre (Mamlakah is Hebrew for "Kingdom") exists to bridge institutional, individual, and situational gaps in ministry preparation. We recognize the gifts and potential of leaders and preachers who may not have had access to formal training, and we provide a holistic, accessible platform for their development.

Core Values
  • Grace and Truth: Guided by the vision of the church, all programs reflect Christ’s character and biblical integrity.
  • Discipleship: Prioritizing spiritual formation and personal growth.
  • Excellence: Striving for the highest standards in education and ministry practice.
  • Empowerment: Equipping every participant for leadership and service.
  • Inclusivity: Welcoming members from all denominations and backgrounds.
  • Mentorship: Connecting learners with seasoned ministers who model godly leadership.
Programs Offered
  • Leadership Development
  • Entrepreneurship and Business Training
  • Community Service and Social Impact
  • Teaching, Preaching, and Public Speaking
  • Biblical Interpretation and Apologetics
  • Pastoral, Apostolic, Prophetic, and Evangelistic Ministry
  • Church Governance and Administration
Methodology
  • Needs-based curriculum design informed by institutional, individual, and situational assessments.
  • Practical, hands-on training coupled with theological foundations.
  • Mentorship from experienced and proven ministry leaders.
  • Opportunities for collaboration and peer learning.
Target Audience

Gifted and sincere leaders, preachers, and aspiring ministers from Kingdom Gathering Church and other denominations who desire to be equipped for fruitful ministry and leadership, regardless of prior formal education.

Our Commitment

At Mamlakah Training Centre, we are committed to raising a new generation of well-equipped, Spirit-led leaders who will represent Christ with integrity, expand the Kingdom, and bring transformation to their communities and beyond.
