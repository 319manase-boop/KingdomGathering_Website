How Your Generosity Makes A Difference

When you give to Kingdom Gathering Church, you are joining a mission that is biblical, community-centered, and deeply rooted in kingdom impact.

  • Host worship services and prayer gatherings that renew hearts.
  • Support community outreach initiatives that bring practical hope.
  • Equip leaders and disciples for faithful service.
  • Reach families across Botswana with the Gospel.
  • Develop future ministry leaders who carry the vision forward.
  • Expand Kingdom impact through church growth and mercy ministry.

Stewardship Statement: We are committed to handling every gift with integrity, accountability, and transparency. Your generosity is carefully stewarded to support the mission and ministry of Kingdom Gathering Church.

Your gift at work

Your generosity sustains worship, equips leaders, supports outreach, cares for families, and helps us proclaim the Gospel in every community we serve.

Every gift is a faithful investment in discipleship, mercy ministry, and the expansion of God’s Kingdom.
