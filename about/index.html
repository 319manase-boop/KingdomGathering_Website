From Vision to Impact

What began as a calling from God has grown into a thriving ministry committed to discipleship, leadership, community transformation, and advancing God's Kingdom across Botswana.

Our Story

A purpose built on prayer, people, and promise.

Kingdom Gathering Church began with a simple obedience to God's call. From humble beginnings at New Disaneng Farms, the ministry has grown into a place where people are welcomed, shaped, and sent into mission with courage and compassion.

Foundation

In 2018, local leaders answered the Lord's invitation to plant a church centered on grace, truth, and relentless prayer.

Growth

Through worship, discipleship, and community care, the church expanded from Maun to reach Serowe, Gaborone, and many neighborhoods beyond.

Impact

Families have been restored, leaders empowered, and communities strengthened by the practical love and kingdom truth of the church.

Future Vision

The journey continues toward a future where every heart touches the gospel and every community experiences transformation through Christ.

Meet the Leaders Behind the Vision

Faithful leaders committed to prayer, discipleship, integrity, and the advancement of God's Kingdom.

Calling & Foundation

Apostle Nonofo Wilfred Mataboge was ordained as a minister of the Gospel in 2003 under the Assemblies of God Church in Botswana. A dedicated leader, he prioritizes human and leadership development as core aspects of church growth and Kingdom expansion. Married to Mrs. Moitshephi Mataboge, they are blessed with two children. In 2017, he answered a new call and founded Kingdom Gathering Church in 2018.

Ministry Journey

Apostle Mataboge brings 23 years of ministry experience to his leadership. Since founding Kingdom Gathering Church, he has led with pastoral care, strategic vision, and practical mentorship. The church has grown significantly: six members have been licensed to preach the Gospel, and he now leads a team of two additional fully ordained pastors serving in Maun and Gaborone. Through worship gatherings, outreach campaigns, discipleship training, and intentional community care, the church continues to expand across Botswana.

Education & Training

A graduate of the Assembly Bible College, he holds a Bachelor of Ministry from Vision International University and an Advanced Certificate in IT and Business Skills from Botho University—combining biblical foundation with practical leadership and technology skills.

Vision for the Church

He envisions a thriving community of disciples sent into mission with courage and compassion. His dream is to see leaders multiplied, communities transformed, and God's Kingdom advanced through Kingdom Gathering across Botswana and beyond.
