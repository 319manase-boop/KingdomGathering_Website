About Kingdom Gathering Church

Founded in Maun, New Disaneng Farms, Botswana, on 21 January 2018, Kingdom Gathering Church has grown to include branches in Serowe and Gaborone. In 2025, the church acquired 11.4 hectares of land for future expansion.

Vision
